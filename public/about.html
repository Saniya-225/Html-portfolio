About Me

I’m a recent Computer Science graduate who loves solving problems through code and creating things that people can actually use. My journey into tech has been shaped by exploring different areas — from learning the basics of software development and cybersecurity to diving deeper with an internship on blockchain and Python. Each step has fueled my curiosity and given me the confidence to take on new challenges in full-stack development.

Along the way, I’ve also built projects that I’m proud of, like a machine learning model for Crime Hotspot Detection that uses data to predict vulnerable areas, and PublicEduChain, a blockchain-based platform that makes educational records more transparent and trustworthy. These experiences taught me not just about technology, but also about building solutions that can make a real difference.
